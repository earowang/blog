ggplot2 has probably been a standard tool to make statistical graphics in the R community over years. If one is asked why using ggplot2, you may receive answers like “it makes graphs look pretty”. ggplot2 is yet more than that. The first two letters gg is the abbreviation of “grammar of graphics”, which implies that there’s statistical thinking underlying plots. The idea/framework originated from Leland Wikinson who wrote the book “The Grammar of Graphics” in 1999, and was further extended and implemented by Hadley Wickham, the author of ggplot2.
